Dead Space’ fans can get a sneak peek at the remake

EA’s Motive Studios can supply an early look at its coming Dead space remake throughout a Twitch stream scheduled for 1PM ET, the developer declared on Mon. Fans of the classic survival horror game will get to visualize a prototype build that Motive said can showcase its vision for the remaster. The studio also promised senior producer Philippe Ducharme and creative director Roman Campos-Oriola are going to be on hand to talk to the work the team at Motive is doing to update the 2008 title for current generation consoles. in addition, the stream can feature members from the Dead space community who are consulting on the project.
Following a series of tantalizing rumors, ea first teased it absolutely was working on a Dead space remake during its recent Play Live event. The minute-long clip the publisher shared showed off an oppressive necromorph-infested environment and protagonist Isaac Clarke. Dead space does not presently have a release date, however it’s heading to PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S and pc.
